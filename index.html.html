📢 S.I.R Rehnumai – Aham Sawalat

1 S.I.R kya hai?
S.I.R Election Commission ki ek muhim hai jisme voter list ko sahi aur update kiya jata hai taake sirf sahi voter ka naam list mein rahe.
2 S.I.R kyun zaroori hai?
Voter list ko durust banane, galat ya duplicate naam hatane aur sahi voter ko vote ka haq dene ke liye.
3 Mapping kya hai?
Mapping ka matlab hai har voter ko uske sahi ghar aur polling booth ke saath jodna.
4 Mapping kyun zaroori hai?
Taake election ke waqt voter ko sahi booth mile aur voter list mein ghalti na ho.
5 Agar Mapping na karein to kya hoga?
Agar voter ki mapping nahi hogi to uska naam Unmapped Category mein ja sakta hai aur future mein vote dene mein mushkil ho sakti hai.
6 Unmapped Category kya hai?
Woh voter jinka address verify nahi hua, BLO ko ghar nahi mila ya mapping mukammal nahi hui.
7 BLO kaun hota hai?
BLO (Booth Level Officer) woh sarkari officer hota hai jo voter list ki verification aur mapping ka kaam karta hai.
8 BLO kaise maloom karein?
Voter Helpline App download karein aur usme "Search BLO" option se naam aur mobile number pata kar sakte hain.
9 Agar 2002 ki voter list mein naam nahi hai to kya karein?
Apne walid ya dada ka naam 2002 ki voter list mein search karein.
10 2002 ki voter list mein naam kaise search karein?
Is link par ja kar apna ya walid ka naam search karein:
ceoelection.maharashtra.gov.in/2002/2002rolldata.aspx
11 S.I.R mein kaun se documents zaroori ho sakte hain?
Aadhaar Card, Voter ID Card, Ration Card, Electricity Bill, Gas Bill, Rent Agreement, Birth Certificate.
12 Unmapped Category se kaise bachein?
Apne BLO se rabta karein, verification ke waqt ghar par maujood rahein, documents dikhayein aur sahi address confirm karein.
13 S.I.R ki sahi maloomat kahan milegi?
BLO, Election Office ya Voter Helpline App se.

📢 Aham Paigham

Agar aap ne abhi tak mapping nahi karwai to jaldi karein — apne BLO se rabta karein aur verification mukammal karein taake future mein vote dene mein koi mushkil na ho.
